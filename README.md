# customer-segmentation-and-behavioral-targeting-analysis
Customer Segmentation and Behavioral Targeting Analysis using Python, MySQL and Power BI

## Goal of the Project
The purpose of this project is to conduct a Customer Segmentation and Behavioral Targeting Analysis using the Online Retail dataset. Customer segmentation was performed through an RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) model, a widely used analytical technique that groups customers based on their purchasing behavior and transaction history.

Customers were segmented into groups such as Champions, Loyal Customers, Potential Customers, At Risk Customers, and Lost Customers to better understand customer value, engagement, and spending patterns. The analysis aims to identify high-value customers, detect customers at risk of churn, and uncover opportunities for targeted marketing strategies.

The project includes data cleaning, KPI analysis, RFM analysis, customer segmentation, revenue analysis, and behavioral profiling using Python. Key insights were further analyzed using MySQL and visualized through an interactive Power BI dashboard. The findings can help businesses improve customer retention, optimize marketing campaigns, increase revenue, and make data-driven customer engagement decisions.

#Tools and Technologies used
Python- Python was used for data cleaning, KPI analysis, customer behavior analysis, and RFM-based customer segmentation. Using Pandas and Matplotlib, key metrics such as revenue, customer value, top customers, top products, and monthly performance were analyzed to generate business insights.

MySQL- MySQL was used to store the final RFM dataset and perform analytical queries on customer segments, revenue contribution, customer distribution, and value groups.

Power BI-Power BI was used to develop an interactive Customer Segmentation and Behavioral Targeting Dashboard, visualizing customer segments, revenue contribution, customer value groups, and marketing insights.

##Analysis Approach

1. Data Cleaning & Preparation
Removed duplicate records and handled missing values.
Created a Revenue column (Quantity × Unit Price).
Filtered invalid transactions and prepared the dataset for analysis.

3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Identified top-selling products and high-revenue countries.
Analyzed customer distribution and purchase behavior.
Explored revenue patterns across customers and transactions.
Detected outliers and negative values in revenue data.

5. KPI Analysis
Calculated Total Revenue, Total Customers, Average Order Value, and Average Customer Value.
Identified top customers and overall business performance indicators.

7. RFM Analysis & Customer Segmentation
Built an RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) model to evaluate customer behavior.
Segmented customers into groups such as Champions, Loyal Customers, Potential Customers, At Risk, and Lost Customers.

9. Behavioral Analysis
Analyzed customer spending patterns and purchase frequency.
Evaluated revenue contribution across different customer segments.
Classified customers into High Value and Low Value groups for targeting strategies.

11. Dashboard Development
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize customer segmentation, revenue insights, and business performance.
